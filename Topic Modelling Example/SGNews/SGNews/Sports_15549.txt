SINGAPORE - He used to terrorise strikers with his hulking, 1.94m-tall frame - and defenders as well, with his fierce on-pitch reprimands at their defensive lapses.
But former Manchester United goalkeeper Peter Schmeichel was a friendly giant at the Jalan Besar Stadium yesterday, when he gave a motivational talk to the national age-group footballers that left them all smiles at his colourful anecdotes.
The 48-year-old Dane, in town as the brand ambassador for Courts, also joined the Young Lions in a Shoot for Hope charity event at the stadium, raising money for the Singapore Disability Sports Council by saving penalty kicks.
