NGHE AN - Dien Chau District's traffic police arrested a man with three frozen tigers in his car on Monday.
Three people were driving on Highway No 1A from central Ha Tinh Province to Ha Noi when the traffic police ordered the driver to pull over. The driver did not stop but turned onto Highway No 48. The car kept running at a high speed until the tires exploded.
The driver and another man fled from the car. The police were able to arrest the only man left and three frozen tigers. They also found four fake licence plates and an iron tube in the car.
