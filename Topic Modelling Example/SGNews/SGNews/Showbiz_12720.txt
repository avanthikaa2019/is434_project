The criminally overlooked Love In A Puff was one of the best films of 2010, so it is with delight that one approaches its sequel.
The good news is that Love In The Buff is a worthy successor.
Listening to his characters talk is one of the greatest pleasures of watching a film by Pang Ho Cheung, a leading light of Hong Kong cinema.
The writer-director has a keen ear for dialogue in the way characters engage in conversation.
LOVE IN THE BUFF (PG13) Romantic comedy/120 minutes Rating: 4/5 stars
