In another report, Metro Ahad said anti-vice officers who raided three budget hotels in Seremban were shocked to find an elderly woman offering sex services.
Negri Sembilan CID chief Asst Comm Hamdan Majid said the 63-year-old was among 25 women, including foreigners, nabbed in the raids on Friday.
Fourteen men believed to be customers as well as three others said to be the caretakers of the premises were also held for questioning.
