WASHINGTON - &ldquo;Multiple victims&rdquo; were reported after a shooting at an apartment complex in the city of Auburn, Alabama, local media said Sunday.
Police officers have put up a covering around the crime scene to shield it from view and protect it from the rain, the Birmingham News newspaper reported. No official number of the victims has been released.
A local television station said the shooting took place late Saturday at University Heights Apartments in the city and involved football players.
ABC 33/40 reported that the apartment complex had been placed in a lockdown, and residents were unable to leave or enter the property until about 1.20 am (0520 GMT, 1.20 Singapore time) Sunday.
