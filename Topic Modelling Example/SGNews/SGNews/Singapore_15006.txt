The Government will release this month six residential sites that can yield about 2,380 private homes.
The sites are being released by the Urban Redevelopment Authority and the Housing Board.
The Government Land Sales Programme for the first half of this year aims to release land for up to 14,100 residential units.
Three land parcels at Sengkang Square, Buangkok Drive and Pasir Ris Drive 3, which can yield about 1,780 housing units, were launched yesterday under the Confirmed List.
Two residential sites at Alexandra View and Farrer Drive were released yesterday under the Reserve List.
The sixth land parcel in Farrer Road will be released for sale under the Reserve List on April 30.
The two Farrer sites can potentially yield about 140 units, while the Alexandra View site is estimated to yield 455.
