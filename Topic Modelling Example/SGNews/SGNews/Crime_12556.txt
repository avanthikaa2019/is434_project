SINGAPORE - A 47-year-old man was arrested for two alleged snatch theft attempts in Jurong West.
The man was struck twice on Friday, May 18 between noon and 1pm.
In his first attempt, he unsuccessfully tried to snatch a mobile phone from a female victim.
However, his second attempt was successful and he got away with a mobile phone and EZ link card from his second victim.
But, he was caught on the same day by the police. The police also recovered the stolen items.
If found guilty he faces a jail term between one and 7 years and is also liable to be caned.
