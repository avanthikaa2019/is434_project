Garmin has launched the Garmin StreetPilot Onboard app for iPhone in Singapore.
Users can download the app directly onto their smartphone and enjoy navigation experience without tapping into their data plan.
The app features are developed by Garmin and leverages the intuitive interface of a Garmin navigation device.
Pre-loaded maps allow for zooming and panning, and even searching for points of interest.
Routes can be planned according to time, distance to destination or next via point and direction of travel.
Garmin StreetPilot Onboard Singapore is available for $29.99 in the Apple App Store.
Garmin plans to launch versions of StreetPilot Onboard for iPhone in additional countries across Asia in the near future.
