Children between the ages of six and 19 who are keen on performing at this year's Child- Aid concert have until May 18 to submit their entries.
Their entries - which can be submitted to childaid@sph. com.sg - must include a video recording of their performance and a short write-up on why they want to be part of the concert on Dec 7 and 8. For details, go to www.straitstimes. com/childaid
