Singapore's oldest cable radio station, Rediffusion, will be going off the air come April 30, reported The Straits Times.
It was reported that the station had announced the end date of its service via e-mail to the papers on Sunday, April 8.
The e-mail had expressed gratitude to its listeners for their support during the station's 63-year journey on the airwaves.
The station started broadcasting in 1949.
