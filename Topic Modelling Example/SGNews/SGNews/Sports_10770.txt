German fans have found a way to annoy fans of opening-group stage opponents Portugal, by shouting "Messi!" when they came across their Portuguese counterparts.
Mention of the Barcelona star is a red rag to a bull for Portugal star Cristiano Ronaldo, who insists he is the man to succeed Lionel Messi as World Player of the Year, despite the Argentinian outscoring him in their two-man scoring duel in the Spanish Primera Liga.
