US model Lily Aldridge and her husband Caleb Followill, the singer of US rock band Kings Of Leon, are celebrating the birth of their baby girl, Dixie Pearl.
The 26-year-old Victoria's Secret beauty gave birth to the couple's first child at the Vanderbilt University Medical Center in Nashville, Tennessee, last week, reported Us Weekly magazine.
Aldridge and Followill, 30, who met at the Coachella Festival in 2007, wed in May last year.
