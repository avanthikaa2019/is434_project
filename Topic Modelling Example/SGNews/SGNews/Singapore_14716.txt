Three men are injured and one is believed to have died after a tugboat exploded at Benoi Road shipyard on Friday morning at 11.40am.
All of them suffered from serious burns. The man who died is believed to have succumbed to his burns.
A local news source said the workers are Indonesians and that they were sent to the National University Hospital (NUH) for treatment.
The Singapore Civil Defence Force said the fire had been put out in the eight minutes they took to arrive.
