A cleaner who stabbed her abusive husband four times after a fight in in Woodlands, Singapore was let off with a fine.
China Press reported that the unemployed husband had punched the 54-year-old woman before slapping her and hitting her body.
He also accused her of cheating on him during the fight which started after she did not go to work on a public holiday.
His wife retaliated by picking up a 10cm-long knife and stabbing him.
However, she immediately alerted her relatives who called the ambulance and police for help.
She pleaded guilty to a charge of grievously hurting her 51-year-old husband under grave and sudden provocation in the incident.
She was fined S$3,000.
