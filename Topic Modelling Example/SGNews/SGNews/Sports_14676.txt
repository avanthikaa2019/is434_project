SINGAPORE - The Singapore National Olympic Council (SNOC) has released a list of nominees for the Singapore Sports Awards this year, but for the first time in its 45-year history, are choosing to keep the results under wraps until the ceremony on May 29.
Swimmer Joseph Schooling, 16, is the favourite to claim the Sportsman of the Year award, reported The Straits Times.
Schooling is up against shooter Zhang Jin.
There will be a four-way tussle for the Women's award, with Fu Mingtian (badminton), Daphne Tan (bowling), Tao Li (swimming) and Feng Tianwei all vying for the prize.
There will also be two new awards this year - The Best Sports Event (International and Local) and Most Inspiring Sports Story of the Year.
