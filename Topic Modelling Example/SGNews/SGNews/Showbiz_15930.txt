POP star Katy Perry will join the entertainment roster for the Formula One Singapore Grand Prix.
Perry - famed for hits like I Kissed A Girl - will help close the third and final day of the race at the Padang stage on Sept 23. Visit www.singaporegp.sg for tickets and details.
Meanwhile, dates for the live show of Cesar Millan, aka the Dog Whisperer, have been announced. Millan, the star of the National Geographic TV series, will present his show at Marina Bay Sands on June 2 and 3. He will give tips for rearing a calm and balanced dog.
Tickets start from $88, and go on sale via Sistic at 10am on Tuesday. StarHub TV customers who subscribe to the Education group can buy their tickets from today.
