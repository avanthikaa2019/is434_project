SINGAPORE - Australian duo Jayesslee will hold another show in Singapore after the 1,600 seats for their May 4 show sold out in a few hours, concert organiser Launch Entertainment announced.
The duo will play another date at the Kallang Theatre on May 5.
Tickets, priced from $38, will go on sale tomorrow at 9am via Sistic (www.sistic.com.sg).
The duo, comprising 25-year-old twins Janice and Sonia Lee, first found fame in 2009 via YouTube and are known for covering songs from the likes of Bruno Mars and The Script.
