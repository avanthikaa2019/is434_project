The Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF) will be conducting a ground deployment exercise at Changi City Point on Thursday, May 24, 2012.
The exercise will last from 5am to 7am.
About 160 personnel and 20 SCDF emergency and supporting vehicles will be participating in this exercise.
During the exercise, SCDF vehicles will also have their beacon lights turned on to alert motorists.
Exercise signages will also be placed at prominent locations to keep the public informed.
The public is advised not to be alarmed.
Such exercises are part of SCDF's on-going efforts to validate and update operational plans and procedures.
