Tickets for the June 19-24 Li-Ning Singapore Open badminton tournament go on sale today at all Sistic outlets, via the Sistic hotline 6348-5555 and at www.sistic.com.sg
Season tickets for the entire tournament are priced from $16-$100 during the early-bird promotional period from today till May 20, while daily tickets are priced from $14-$58.
