Sin Chew Daily reported that Taiwanese pop star Jay Chou denied that he had attacked the paparazzi and stated that he had merely protected himself.
According to witnesses at the scene, one photographer grabbed Chou's hand causing the singer to push the photographer away.
The photographer was said to have grabbed Chou's hand when the singer tried to take photos of the paparazzi.
The incident happened when Chow was having dinner with his mother, model Hannah Quinlivan and some 20 friends in Taipei last Thursday.
