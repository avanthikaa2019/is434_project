South Indian actress Meera Jasmine, 28, has denied marrying her boyfriend Mandolin Rajesh.
"There is no need to have a secret wedding," she was quoted as saying.
"We will announce it openly when we decide to tie the knot."
Meera said the wedding would take place with the blessings of both families.
Some of Meera's memorable movies are Geethai, Anjaneya and Sandaikozhi.
