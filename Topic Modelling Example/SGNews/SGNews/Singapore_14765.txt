The driver of a car sustained serious head injuries in an accident in the Sungei Kadut area on Wednesday.
A truck collided with the car at the junction of Sungei Kadut Street 3 and Sungei Kadut Drive at 2.37pm. The car driver, 58, was unconscious when he was sent to the Khoo Teck Puat Hospital.
Witnesses can call the Traffic Police on 1800-547-1818.
