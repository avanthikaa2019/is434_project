SINGAPORE - Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong spent a few hours in Hougang on Saturday, May 19, with fellow People's Action Party (PAP) member and candidate for the single member constituency (SMC), Desmond Choo.
PM Lee and Desmond Choo visited residents in their homes, reported The Straits Times.
The Prime Minister also visited the Sunlove Seniors Activities Centre at Block 1, Hougang Avenue 3.
Mr Desmond Choo is contesting in the by-election for the single-seat constituency.
The People's Action Party will be holding their election rally at the Hougang Stadium on May 20, 7pm.
