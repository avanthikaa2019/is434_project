Fans of noughties-style hard rock will enjoy the full-on musical assault of this American band's fourth album.
But so run-of-the-mill are songs like the title track and lead single Bully that they wouldn't be out of place as some cheesy pro-wrestler's entrance song.
Surprise, surprise: Adrenaline, with its pummelling drumline, has been named the official theme of World Wrestling Entertainment's Extreme Rules.
Shinedown Amaryllis (Warner) 2.5/5 stars
