SINGAPORE - Singapore's men have finished in eighth place at the world team table-tennis championships.
They first lost 1-3 to Sweden in their fifth to eighth placing play-off.
Yang Zi played against Swedish No. 3 Jens Lundquist in the first match, and lost in three tight games.
Gao Ning levelled the scores in a five-match thriller against Swedish veteran Jorgen Persson.
