SINGAPORE - A man, 59, was charged in court with molesting a 12-year-old girl on two occassions in 2010.
Shu Hee Tong is accused of placing the girl's hand on his thigh while they were on board a bus travelling along Hougang Avenue 8.
This happened sometime in August 2010.
Shu also alleged outraged her modesty by allegedly showing her a video of a naked woman on his mobile phone outside a flat in Hougang.
The Straits Times reported that in October 2010, he had texted her asking her to have sex with him at a hotel room.
The case is adjourned till April 23.
