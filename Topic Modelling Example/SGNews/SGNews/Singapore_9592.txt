Dressed in a Punjabi suit, Mr Pritam Singh, 35, MP for Aljunied GRC, got married yesterday.
A photo of the groom, flanked by Workers' Party well-wishers Low Thia Khiang and Sylvia Lim and other parliamentary colleagues, was shared by fellow MP Chen Show Mao on his Facebook page. The photo was "liked" more than 800 times.
Mr Chen wished "a lifetime of domestic bliss to Loveleen and Pritam on the happy occasion of their wedding".
