SINGAPORE - Members of the public are invited to watch the changing of Istana Guards Ceremony to be held this Sunday at 6pm.
Held by the Singapore Armed Forces (SAF) Military Police Command, the event will occur at the Istana main gate.
The new Guards will march along Orchard Road towards the Istana to take over from the old Guards.
The Singapore Armed Forces Bands A &amp; B will be in attendance.
