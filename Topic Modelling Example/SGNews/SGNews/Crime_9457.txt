A 39-year-old woman was on her way home along Boundary Close on June 23 when a man covered her mouth and grabbed her waist.
The victim put up a violent struggle and the suspect retaliated by punching her before fleeing the scene.
The police arrested the suspect on June 25 in the vicinity of Macpherson road and preliminary investigations revealed that it was a case of robbery with hurt.
The suspect will be charged in court on June 27 for the offence of robbery with hurt.
If convicted, he will face up to 20 years jail and up to 12 strokes of the cane.
The police advises members of the public to adopt these following measures if confronted:
1. Remain calm and take note of the description of the perpetrator, e.g. his appearance, clothing, height and other physical details;
2. Note the direction and mode of escape by the perpetrator; and
3. Call the police as soon as it is safe to do so.
