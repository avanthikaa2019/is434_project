Police arrested a 27-year-old man believed to be involved in a case of snatch theft committed at Jalan Rumah Tinggi.
On May 20, 2012 at about 9.00pm, the police received a report where the suspect had snatched away a mobile phone valued at about $200 which belonged to a 40-year-old female victim in the vicinity of Jalan Rumah Tinggi.
The suspect was traced and arrested at Serangoon Road on May 22.
The suspect will be charged in court for snatch theft. If convicted, he faces a jail term of up to seven years and caning.
