In this Spanish mixed bag of horror, romance and dark comedy, the circus is used as a metaphor for the equally absurd world outside the tents.
Two clowns (Carlos Areces, Antonio de la Torre) lock horns over a beautiful acrobat (Carolina Bang) and cause disastrous consequences.
It's a pity writer-director Alex de la Iglesia lets the story spin out of control with in-your-face violence and bizarre scenarios which distract from rather than reinforce underlying messages.
