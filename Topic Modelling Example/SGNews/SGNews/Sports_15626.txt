AUGUSTA, Georgia - Bubba Watson of the United States won the 76th Masters at Augusta National on Sunday on the second hole of a sudden-death playoff against Louis Oosthuizen of South Africa.
The two finished the 72 holes at 10-under par 278.
Two strokes further back on 280 in a share of second place were a quartet comprising Lee Westwood of England (68), Americans Matt Kuchar (69) and Phil Mickelson (72) and Peter Hanson of Sweden (73).
It was his first win in a major and he is just the third left-hander to win the Masters.
