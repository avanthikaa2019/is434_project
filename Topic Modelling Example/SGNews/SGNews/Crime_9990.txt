Police are investigating a 29-year-old man for making a false report claiming he was robbed along the footpath near Block 229, Jurong East Street 21, last Saturday.
The man, who works as an IT engineer, claimed to have been robbed by an unknown person, who assaulted him and made off with his laptop valued at about $1,500. Investigations revealed that the man had made the false report to cover up the loss of the laptop, which belonged to his company.
