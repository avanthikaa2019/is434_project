Singapore began their South- east Asia Basketball Association Under-18 men's championship with a 123-19 thrashing of Laos on Tuesday night at the Singapore Basketball Centre.
The championship was opened by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister for Finance Tharman Shanmugaratnam, with five countries vying for a position to represent South-east Asia in the Asia U-18 Champion- ship to be held in Ulan Bator, Mongolia, later this year.
