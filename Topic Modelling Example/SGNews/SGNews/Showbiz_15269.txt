This unique, nine-piece homegrown outfit clearly have their sights set beyond Singapore shores.
If this single is anything to go by - it's effervescent, radio- friendly and darn catchy - they're after intergalactic domination.
Singer Aarika Lee's airy vocals contrast with rapper Kevin Lester's no-nonsense flow on B-side What If It's You, giving the funkalicious song a meatier feel.
