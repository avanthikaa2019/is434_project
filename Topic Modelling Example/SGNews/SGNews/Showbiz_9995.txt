HONG KONG - China Press reported that a month after Hong Kong celebrities Nicholas Tse and Cecilia Cheung were officially divorced, the couple were rumoured to be back together.
Quoting Hong Kong's 3-Weekly magazine, it said Tse and Cheung's elder son Lucas played Cupid in helping to reconcile the couple.
It was reported that when Tse went for lunch with his children, Lucas suddenly blurted out: "I love to have meal with papa and mama together very much!"
