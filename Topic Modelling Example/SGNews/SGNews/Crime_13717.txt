SINGAPORE - Police are looking for the man shown in the above picture to assist with investigations into a case of outrage of modesty.
The crime occurred on April 29 in the vicinity of Hougang Avenue 4.
He is described to be a male in his 20s of Malay or Indian descent.
He is 1.65m tall, of slim build with short black hair and a dark complexion.
At the time of the event, he was clad in a blue tee-shirt and dark-coloured bermudas.
Anyone with information is requested to call the Police Hotline at 1800-255-0000.
The police assured the public that all information will be kept strictly confidential.
