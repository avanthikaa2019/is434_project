SINGAPORE - South Korean singer-actor Kim Hyun Joong will be holding his first-ever fan meet here on May 4, reported The Straits Times.
Singapore fans will get first dibs of Kim's latest concert as it will be the first leg of his Asian Tour.
Those attending the meet can expect to see the Korean heartthrob perform songs from his latest solo albums, Break Down and Lucky.
Event organiser Running Into The Sun, which made the announcement on Thursday, promised that the concert will be 'fresh and electrifying'.
Kim rose to fame as leader of K-pop group SS501 and for his role in the popular Korean drama Boys Over Flowers.
Concert tickets will go on sale via Sistic within the next two weeks, said the report.
