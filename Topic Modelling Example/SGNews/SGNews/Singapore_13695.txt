SINGAPORE - The Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), has appointed Emeritus Senior Minister Goh Chok Tong as its patron of advancement, where he will be involved in fund-raising for the university.
This is the first time he has taken on such a role at a university, reported The Straits Times.
The appointment was announced by SUTD president Thomas Magnanti during a gala on Friday evening, to raise funds for scholarships and bursaries.
The event raised a total of $10 million, after the Government matched all proceeds three-to-one.
