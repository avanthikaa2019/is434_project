Police are appealing for information on an unidentified body found floating at sea off Keppel Harbour on May 16 at about 2.25pm.
The deceased is a 1.7m tall male believed to be in his 20s to 30s and is of stout build. He had black short hair and a fair complexion.
He was found wearing a black T-shirt with a Halong Bay, Vietnam print, a pair of black long pants with a brown leather belt and black sports shoes with white socks.
Anyone with information is requested to call the police hotline at 1800-255-0000.
