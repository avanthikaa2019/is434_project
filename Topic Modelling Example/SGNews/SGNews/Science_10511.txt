Rockstar Games on Tuesday confirmed that third-person shooter video game Max Payne for Android will be available from today.
Optimised for Android devices, the game features HD graphics, high-resolution textures, Social Club connectivity, user-customisable controls, and gamepad support for select USB controllers, said Rockstar in a blog post.
The game debuts in the Google Play store for US$2.99 (S$3.82). The iOS version is already available at the same price in the Apple App Store
