THE Stone Roses, who reunited last year, will bring their reunion tour to the Singapore Indoor Stadium on July 22. Original members Ian Brown, John Squire, Gary Mounfield and Alan Wren will be here to play their first Singapore gig.
The iconic group is known for hits like She Bangs The Drums and I Wanna Be Adored.
Tickets - from $68 to $128 - go on sale tomorrow at 9am via Sistic (www.sistic.com.sg, tel: 6348-5555).
