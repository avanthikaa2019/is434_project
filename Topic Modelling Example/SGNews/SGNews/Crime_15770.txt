South Sulawesi Police are hunting down DN, a resident of Makassar, for allegedly having impregnated two of his stepdaughters.
DN's crime was revealed after the eldest daughter, IL, 16, gave birth, leaving her sister, NH, 14, who was by then seven months' pregnant with her stepfather's child.
DN was also reportedly about to rape the youngest daughter, HF, 13, metrotvnews.com reported.
Upon hearing the report, residents raided DN's home but DN along with his wife, WH, managed to escape.
According to the victims, they had told their mother, WH, about DN's crimes, but WH only cowered in fear of being killed by DN.
Makassar Police detectives are now searching for DN and WH, while the three sisters are being cared for by neighbors.
