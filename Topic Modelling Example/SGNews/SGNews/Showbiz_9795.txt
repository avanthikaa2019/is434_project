On the spoken-word skit Classical Teacher, Jack Black declares that "being almost as good as Arcade Fire" isn't good enough.
Yes, no one would dispute that nobody does comedy-rock as well as the actor and his musical partner-in-crime, Kyle Gass.
And, though the no-holdsbarred hilarity does not come as fast and furious as before on their third album, the competent, even technically impressive, songs still succeed at raising eyebrows and making everyone uncomfortable - unless you've a sense of humour to match theirs, of course.
There are tamer songs too but, really, the strait-laced would do well to stay far, far away.
