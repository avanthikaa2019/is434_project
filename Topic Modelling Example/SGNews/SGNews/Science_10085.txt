Singapore - Singapore Press Holdings (SPH) is gearing up to spread its dominance in print classifieds to the online space.
Beginning with the revamped job vertical STJobs (www.STJobs.sg) on June 17, SPH will release its improved offerings in stages. Previously known as ST701Jobs, STJobs has improved on its navigation and use, allowing users to intuitively surf the site.
It promises enhanced features and functionality, providing users with better search results and effective matches. with the "Resume Scan" feature, job seekers can upload their resume in one simple step.
Since its soft launch in March, STJobs has garnered close to 10 million page views, 1.5 million visitors monthly and more than 35,000 Facebook fans. Marketing Magazine named it the No. 1 Recruitment Site in the Digital Media of the Year Awards 2011.
