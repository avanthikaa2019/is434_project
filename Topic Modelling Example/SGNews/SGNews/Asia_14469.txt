JAKARTA - The start of the National Examination for junior high school students on Monday has seen 1,335 police deployed in Jakarta to safeguard against any problems.
"The 1,335 officers will be divided into several groups to secure the distribution of the examination's papers and to guard the schools. Each school is being guarded by one police officer," Jakarta Police spokesman Sr. Comr. Rikwanto said, as quoted by tribunenews.com.
The exams run from April 23 to 26.
