SINGAPORE - Police arrested two teenagers suspected of stealing a motorcycle on Wednesday, March 28.
The officers from Bedok Police Division spotted the two boys, aged 15 and 17, trying to lift a Honda CV600 motorcycle off the ground.
In a statement to the media, the police said that once questioned, the teenagers admitted trying to steal the motorcycle as they realised the key was in the bike's ignition.
Both suspects will be charged in Court on April 2, 2012 for the theft of a motor vehicle with common intention. If convicted, they face a jail term of up to 7 years and a fine.
