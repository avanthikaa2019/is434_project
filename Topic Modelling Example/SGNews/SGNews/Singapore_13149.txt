Sin Chew Daily reported that a survey conducted by advertising agency JWT showed that 10 per cent of the 300 Singaporeans interviewed admitted to surfing social networking sites while being intimate with their partners.
According to the report, 2 per cent of them would also surf social networking sites while breastfeeding.
The survey was conducted between Feb 1 and Feb 8 on 900 Singaporeans, Chinese and Americans (300 from each country) aged between 18 and 26.
In Singapore, 96 per cent of working adults said social networking sites were "very important" to them.
