Thailand's Minister of Defence, Air Chief Marshal (ACM) Sukumpol Suwanatat, called on Minister for Defence Dr Ng Eng Hen at the Ministry of Defence on Friday morning.
During the call, the Ministers reaffirmed the strong defence ties and close cooperation between the two defence establishments.
ACM Sukumpol, who is in Singapore on an introductory visit from May 31 to June 3, will also call on Deputy Prime Minister and Minister for Home Affairs Teo Chee Hean later this morning and on Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong tomorrow.
ACM Sukumpol will also attend the 11th Shangri-La Dialogue this weekend.
The Singapore Armed Forces and the Royal Thai Armed Forces interact regularly through visits, professional exchanges, courses and exercises.
