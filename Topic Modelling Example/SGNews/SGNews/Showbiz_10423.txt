Move over, KISS.
The normally clean-cut Josh Duhamel, 39, showed up at the premiere of movie musical Rock Of Ages last week in true rockstar fashion - even though he was not involved in the film.
The US actor was unrecognisable in his ensemble - which comprised big hair, shades, black leather jacket over bare chest and a half-full bottle of beer, all of which cemented his "rock-star" credentials.
For once, all eyes were on him instead of his pop-star wife, Fergie, who was in a tight black-and-gold dress.
