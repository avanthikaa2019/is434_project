INDIA - A man sold off his two-month-old nephew for 50,000 rupees ($1,220) after he took the baby out.
Apparently, he took the boy out on Friday evening.
Several hours later, he came home without the baby and when questioned by the parents, he merely said the baby had gone missing.
He could not explain how he lost the baby, Tamil Nesan reported.
Police managed to find the baby in Kolkata on Sunday evening.
A woman had been detained.
The baby's uncle went missing after his parents reported to police about their missing child.
