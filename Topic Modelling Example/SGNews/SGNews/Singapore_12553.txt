SINGAPORE - The People's Action Party (PAP) will be holding their by-election rally on Sunday, May 20.
The really will be held in Hougang Stadium at Hougang Ave 2 between 7pm to 10pm.
PAP candidate Desmond Choo, 34, will be addressing the Hougang residents.
Guests speakers include Deputy Prime Minister Teo Chee Hean, MP for Moulmein-Kallang GRC Phua Lay Peng and former MP Peh Chin Hua.
The Deputy Director of Industrial Relations, NTUC, spent a few hours together with Singapore Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong meeting the residents of Hougang on Saturday, May 19.
