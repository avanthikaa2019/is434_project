SEOUL - Hollywood celebrities Will Smith and Ewan McGregor visited Korea on Monday - one to promote his new film the other for a department store event.
Smith, who arrived in Incheon with his wife and a daughter, had a press conference in Seoul to promote the upcoming move "Men In Black 3."
McGregor was recently picked as a model for Shinsegae Department Store commercials.
After a press conference with local reporters at a hotel in the morning, he plans to meet his fans at an autograph-signing event and attend the department store's evening party.
