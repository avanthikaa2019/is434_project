SINGAPORE - She was hit by a bus and became mentally-impaired.
For that, the High Court increased the amount of damages awarded to Madam Tan Juay Mui, 54, to $1 million. She can also return to court for more money within the next five years.
Madam Tan, then 48, was knocked down by a private bus at a pedestrian crossing in Yishun on June 15, 2006, reported The Straits Times.
