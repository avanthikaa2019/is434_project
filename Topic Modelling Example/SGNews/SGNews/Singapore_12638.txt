Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong and Member of Parliament for the Ang Mo Kio GRC giving the finishing touches to a kolam at the Cheng San Community Club on May 12 as part of the Indian New Year celebrations, called Rhythms in Unity.
More than 700 residents, grassroots leaders and fellow Ang Mo Kio GRC MPs Ang Hin Kee, Inderjit Singh and Seng Han Thong turned up for the celebrations.
Mr Lee said events like this can help young Indians understand their roots, give an opportunity for new citizens and immigrants to bond with locals, as well as let other communities understand and enjoy the rich Indian culture.
The event was organised by the Indian Activities Executive Committees of Ang Mo Kio GRC and Sengkang West SMC.
