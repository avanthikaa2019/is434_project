SINGAPORE - Minister for Transport Mr Lui Tuck Yew has appointed 12 members to the Civil Aviation Authority of Singapore (CAAS) Board for the period from July 1, 2012 to June 30, 2015.
The CAAS Board provides strategic direction to grow a safe, vibrant air hub and civil aviation system in Singapore.
It will be chaired by Mr Lee Hsien Yang, Chairman, Fraser &amp; Neave, Ltd.
Board members include Mr Khoo Chin Hean, Chief Executive Officer, OpenNet Pte Ltd, Mr Lee Boon Ngiap, Assistant Managing Director (Banking &amp; Insurance), Monetary Authority of Singapore, Mr Lim Yeow Khee, Managing Director, LYK Aerospace (S) Pte Ltd, MG Ng Chee Meng, Chief of Air Force, Ministry of Defence, Mr Quek Keng Liang, Chairman, Singapore Logistics Association.
Prof Wee Chow Hou, Professor of Strategy and Marketing, Nanyang Business School, Nanyang Technological University, Mr Yap Ong Heng, Director-General, Civil Aviation Authority of Singapore, Ms Cham Hui Fong, Joanne,Assistant Secretary-General, NTUC, Mr Goh Joon Seng, Consultant, M/s Lee &amp; Lee, Mr Yee Ping, Chief Executive Officer, Central Provident Fund Board and Mr Zulkifli Baharudin Managing Director, Global Business Integrators Pte Ltd are also on the Board.
