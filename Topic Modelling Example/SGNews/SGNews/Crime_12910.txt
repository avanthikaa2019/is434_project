PETALING JAYA - A senior district police officer in Selangor has lost his gun to two snatch thieves.
It is learnt that the incident occurred at about 10pm on Saturday in the Seri Kembangan area when the Criminal Investigations Department (CID) chief officer had just finished dinner and was walking to his car.
Two men on a motorcycle approached him and one of them grabbed his sling bag which contained the pistol before speeding off.
The officer was unhurt but he also lost other personal belongings kept in the bag.
He alerted police and subsequently lodged a report.
Selangor CID chief Senior Asst Comm Mohd Adnan Abdullah confirmed the incident, adding that the gun had yet be recovered and the suspects were still at large.
Separately, a 26-year-old man is fighting for his life after he was assaulted and stabbed by unknown assailants near a hotel in Jalan Kelang Lama, Kuala Lumpur.
It is learnt that the man was attacked at Jalan Sepadu at about 7pm yesterday.
Members of the public called the police who arrived within minutes.
It is also learnt that police picked up two men at the scene for questioning while the injured man was rushed to the University Malaya Medical Centre (UMMC).
Brickfields OCPD Asst Comm Wan Abdul Bari Wan Abdul Khalid confirmed the incident.
