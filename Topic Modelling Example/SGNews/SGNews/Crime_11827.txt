SINGAPORE - A 21-year-old suspect, who is believed to have molested a female victim in the lift at the housing estate of Dover Crescent, has been arrested.
Police received a call on May 4 at about 10.25pm from a member of the public, notifying them of the case.
Through ground enquiries and follow-up investigations, police officers managed to establish the identity of the suspect and arrested him in the vicinity of Bukit Batok Street 52 on May 28 at about 11.30 am.
Investigations are ongoing.
If convicted for the offence of outrage of modesty, the suspect may be jailed up to two years and will also be liable to caning.
