SINGAPORE - Police Sunday arrested two youths, aged 12 and 13, for attempting to break into a residential unit in the vicinity of West Coast Walk.
A security guard had called the police at about 1.13am on Sunday to report an attempted break-in at the unit. He said t he suspects escaped through a side entrance of the unit when he tried to confront them.
Officers from Clementi police division managed to establish suspects' identities through reviewing CCTV footage.
They traced the 12-year-old male and arrested him at about 3.30am at West Coast Road. His accomplice was picked up along Clementi Avenue 5 at 11am.
The pair will be charged in court on Monday, April 23 for attempted housebreaking. If convicted they each face three years' in jail and a fine.
