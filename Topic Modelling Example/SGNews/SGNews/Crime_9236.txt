SINGAPORE - Police are looking for this man to assist with investigations of loanshark harassment case.
In a statement, the police said that the incident happened on June 27 at Jurong West Street 81.
The man is described as medium built and fair in complexion.
He was last seen wearing a collared T-shirt and knee-length shorts. He was also carrying a black-coloured sling bag.
Anyone with information is requested to call the Police Hotline at 1800-2550000. All information will be kept strictly confidential.
