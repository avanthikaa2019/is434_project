A lawyer was charged in court for paying an underage Vietnamese girl for sex.
Gwee Hak Theng, 59, is a former deputy public prosecutor who runs his own practice.
He allegedly paid $300 for the sexual services of the 16-year-old last year on July 19.
The Vietnamese teen was part of a vice syndicate operated by a couple in Geylang.
So far, 11 men have been charged in relation to this syndicate.
Two other men have been convicted and jailed for paid sex with underage Vietnamese girls.
Gwee will return to court on July 27.
If convicted, he faces up to seven years in jail and/or a fine.
