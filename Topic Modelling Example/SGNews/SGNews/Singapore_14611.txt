Singapore's Deputy Prime Minister Teo Chee Hean will be in Brunei Darussalam for an official visit from April 23 to 25.
The Coordinating Minister for National Security and Minister for Home Affairs will have an audience with His Majesty Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah, Mu'izzaddin Waddaulah and other members of the royal family.
He will also meet with his counterpart, Minister of Home Affairs Pehin Dato Ustaz Haji Badaruddin Othman.
DPM Teo's visit underscores the excellent bilateral ties and security cooperation between Singapore and Brunei.
The visit will enable him to reaffirm and strengthen these relations and sustain the development of ongoing exchanges between the law enforcement and security agencies of both countries.
This will be DPM Teo's first visit to Brunei Darussalam in his capacity as Minister for Home Affairs.
DPM Teo is accompanied by his wife and senior officials from the Ministry of Home Affairs and its departments.
