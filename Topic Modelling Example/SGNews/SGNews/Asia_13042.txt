TOKYO - Seven people are in a serious condition after a fire at a Japanese hotel on Sunday morning, the local fire department said.
&ldquo;Ten people were taken to hospital,&rdquo; said a local fire department official, adding that seven were unconscious.
The fire started at a hotel in Fukuyama, 600 kilometres (373 miles) west of Tokyo, in Hiroshima prefecture, the official said.
&ldquo;The fire is already under control, and rescuers are checking if any more people are still in the building,&rdquo; he said.
