SINGAPORE - Police have arrested a 29-year-old man believed to be responsible for a case of theft of lorry in the vicinity of Penjuru Close.
The 29-year-old driver who parked his lorry at Penjuru close had reported his vehicle stolen on April 11.
Officers from the Clementi Police Division managed to establish the identity of the suspect, and he was arrested on April 20.
The suspect is also believed to be involved in a separate case on March 2012.
He will be charged in Court tomorrow for Theft of Motor Vehicle under Section 379A of the Penal Code.
If convicted, he faces a punishment of imprisonment for a term of not less than one year and not more than seven years and shall also be liable to a fine.
