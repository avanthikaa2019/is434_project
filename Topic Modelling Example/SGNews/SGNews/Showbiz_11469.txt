Doll-faced starlet Nur Fazura Sharifuddin or Faz has denied receiving sponsorship to set up her boutique, House of Doll, insisting that every sen she put into HOD was from own sheer hard work.
The Gol &amp; Gincu actress told Harian Metro that it had taken her two years to plan and save for her shop in Jalan Telawi 3, Bangsar.
"This business is now my main priority and acting will have to take a back seat for awhile."
