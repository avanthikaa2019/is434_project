SINGAPORE - The police are appealing for information on a hit-and-run accident which happened along Woodlands Avenue 3 on Friday morning.
The accident took place in front of Block 173 along the road towards Causeway Point at about 6am.
An unknown taxi is believed to have failed to stop after hitting a 26-year-old male pedestrian who was crossing the road. The taxi was last seen heading towards the direction of Woodlands Avenue 5.
The pedestrian sustained serious head injuries and was sent to Khoo Teck Puat Hospital.
Anyone with information is requested to call the Traffic Police Hotline at 1800-5471818. All information will be kept strictly confidential.
