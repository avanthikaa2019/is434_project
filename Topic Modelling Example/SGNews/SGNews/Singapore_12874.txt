SINGAPORE - Four persons have been issued with political donation certificates by the Elections Department.
They are - PAP's candidate Mr Desmond Choo; Mr Png Eng Huat, the Workers' Party candidate; Mr Poh Lee Guan, a veteran member of the Workers' Party and independent Zeng Guoyan.
To be a valid candidate for election, the nominee will have to submit to the Returning Officer on Nomination Day a political donation certificate issued by the Registrar of Political Donations.
According to a statement by the Elections Department on Tuesday, it received four applications for the Hougang by-election and issued certificates to all of them.
