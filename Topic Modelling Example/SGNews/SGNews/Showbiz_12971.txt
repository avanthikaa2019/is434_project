Major Chinese dailies reported that Hong Kong superstar Andy Lau had stopped people, including his family and friends, from visiting his newborn baby at the Hong Kong Sanatorium and Hospital in Happy Valley.
Andy has yet to officially confirm the birth of his baby girl.
Guang Ming Daily reported that Lau had suggested to his parents and friends not to visit the baby who was born through Caesarean section on May 8.
The daily reported that Lau had been at the bedside of his Malaysian wife Carol Chu and their baby at the hospital round-the-clock.
It is learnt that Lau had turned down all baby gifts from his friends, fans and sponsors and also rejected numerous advertisements related to baby products.
