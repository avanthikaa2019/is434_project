Live concerts will be part of the lineup to commemorate the opening of the Bay South Garden.
Grammy Award winner Jason Mraz and Singaporean singer-songwriter Corrinne May will perform at The Meadow, a 2ha lawn with standing room for 30,000 people.
Tickets will go on sale April 20.
The events from June 29 to July 8 also include carnivals, outdoor movie screenings and a photography competition.
