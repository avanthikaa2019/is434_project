A fatal road accident took place at Lower Delta road last night.
A male cyclist was run over by a car and pinned under the vehicle.
A driver who witnessed the accident sent video footage of the scene to citizen journalism website Stomp after recording it from his in-car camera.
The accident occurred in the centre lane of the road, causing traffic to slow down.
A spokesperson from the the police said:
"The police are investigating a fatal road accident that took place at about 9.30pm along Lower Delta Road towards Bukit Merah.
"The accident involved a car and a bicycle.
"The cyclist was a 57-year-old Chinese man. He was pronounced dead at 9.55pm and the driver was arrested.
Police investigations are ongoing.
