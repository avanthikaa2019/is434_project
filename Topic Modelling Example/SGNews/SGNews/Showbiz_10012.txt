The Hi-5 gang will be making their way back to the stage in Singapore from September 4 to September 9.
Following their first tour in 2009, they will be returning with their latest stage show "Hi-5 Holiday" with a new cast member, Dayen.
Expect the performance to be bright and funny, and packed with upbeat songs and dancing.
Hi-5 is one of the top three most successful pre-school television shows in Australia and has held this position for the past 10 years.
With their engaging cast, they've captivated children between the ages of two and six with over 13 successful series to date.
Tickets start at $40 and are currently on sale at Marina Bay Sands Box Offices, through www.MarinaBaySands.com/Ticketing and by phone at 6688 8826.
