Five men and two women between the ages of 23 and 68 were arrested by the police for suspected involvement in loansharking activities.
The suspects were arrested in Jurong West Avenue 5 and Woodlands Avenue 6.
The police reported that the suspects had opened bank accounts and given away their Automated Teller Machine (ATM)cards and Personal Identification Numbers (PIN) to loansharking syndicates to facilitate their business.
If found guilty for involvement in loansharking activities as a first time offender, the suspects can be fined between $30,000 and $300,000 and face a jail term of up to four years.
Male offenders can also receive up to six strokes of the cane.
