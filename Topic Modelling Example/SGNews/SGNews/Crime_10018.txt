A 22 year old man has been arrested for suspected involvement in loanshaking activities on 18 June at around 8.15pm.
He was caught in the area of Chulia Street by officers from the Jurong Police Division.
Found within his possession was cash amounting to $700 and one handphone with debtors' information.
During the preliminary investigations, he has been suspected as being a debtor and being involved with loansharks syndicates in the collection of repayment loans from clients.
Investigations against the suspect are ongoing.
