ALOR SETAR - A masseuse alleged that she was beaten up by a policeman while in custody.
Lay Suet Peng, 42, from Kampung Cina here, alleged that she was taken into custody after being arrested during a raid at a massage parlour in Taman Rakyat.
She said a team of plainclothes policemen raided the premises at 5.30pm on April 14 and detained her, a cashier and a male customer after they found the place operating without a permit.
