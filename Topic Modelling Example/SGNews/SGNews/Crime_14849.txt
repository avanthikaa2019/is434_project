SINGAPORE - In just two days, the Central Narcotics Bureau (CNB) arrested four suspected drug traffickers and one suspected drug abuser. Two kilogrammes of heroin estimated to be worth about S$290,000 were also seized.
In one operation on April 16, three male Singaporeans, aged 37, 46 and 50, and one 27-year-old female Singaporean were arrested.
In a separate joint operation with the Immigration &amp; Checkpoints Authority, CNB officers arrested a 23-year-old male Malaysian motorcyclist at the Woodlands checkpoint.
Officers found about 920g of heroin on the motorcycle.
He is being investigated for drug importation. If convicted, he may face the death penalty.
