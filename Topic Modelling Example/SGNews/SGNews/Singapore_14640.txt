SINGAPORE - Australian Prime Minister Julia Gillard will be making an official visit to Singapore from April 22 to 24, at the invitation of Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong.
Ms Gillard will be accompanied by her partner, Mr Tim Mathieson, and officials from the Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet and the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade, according to a statement from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA).
This will be Prime Minister Gillard's first official visit to Singapore.
During her visit, Ms Gillard will call on Acting President JY Pillay and Prime Minister Lee. Prime Minister Lee and Mrs Lee will also host an official dinner in honour of Prime Minister Gillard and Mr Mathieson.
