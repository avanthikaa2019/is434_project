WASHINGTON - A magnitude 6.4 earthquake struck northern Argentina early on Monday, the United States Geological Survey (USGS) said.
The quake occurred at 2.07am local time (1.07pm Singapore time) and was centred 116 km east south-east of Santiago del Estero, the USGS said.
Police in the town said there were no immediate reports of injuries or damage from the quake, which was upgraded from an initial 6.3.
