SINGAPORE - Police are seeking the public's help to identify a man allegedly involved in a loanshark harassment case.
The medium-built man is between the ages of 35 and 45 and was last seen on April 15 in Serangoon Central Drive.
He was wearing a dark t-shirt, bermudas and a dark cap on that day.
Anyone who is able to help can call the Police Hotline at 1800-2550000. All information will be kept strictly confidential.
