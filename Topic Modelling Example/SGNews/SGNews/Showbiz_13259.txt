SINGER Nurul has decided to end her days as a single mother to marry her French lover, Harian Metro reported.
The 35-year-old singer, who has a teenage son, told the paper that she met Eric Daniel Depp, 47, two years ago and they have decided to tie the knot in Kuala Lumpur on June 6.
The artiste, whose real name is Nurul Huda Abdul Wahab, said his family had "graciously accepted me into their fold".
"Nothing is stopping me from building a home for the second time with someone who has earned a place in my heart," added Nurul, who also writes songs and hosts TV shows.
She was once married to composer Mohd Faizal Maas better known as Ajai. They divorced in 2007 after 11 years of marriage.
