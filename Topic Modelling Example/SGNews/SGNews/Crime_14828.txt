MALAYSIA - A 51-year-old technician has been detained by police 10 times because he resembles a con man, Sin Chew Daily reported.
Xiao Guo Cai from Teluk Intan said he hopes the con man will stop cheating people.
"Enough is enough. If he cheats one more time, I will be detained again," said Xiao who has a nine-year-old daughter.
Xiao was detained by police in Tapah, Ipoh and Manjung between April 3 and 12.
The daily reported that the con man had been cheating people in Perak since 2008.
Xiao said his reputation has been damaged and his work disrupted whenever he is detained by police.
