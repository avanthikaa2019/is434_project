Harian Metro reported that 36-year-old actor Aaron Aziz had denied that a picture of a MyKad showing the name Mat Haron Mustapha Mat Aziz Mustafa was actually his.
He said he was shocked when he read a post on a popular entertainment forum claiming that he had lied to his fans about his real name.
Maintaining that his real name is Aaron Aziz Mustafa, the popular drama actor claimed that someone had forged the picture.
