After its inaugural run at Tiong Bahru Park last November, home-grown music festival Lepa(r)k! is back.
To be held on a bigger scale, the event - a collaboration between Tanglin-Cairnhill Citizens' Consultative Committee, Incursive Productions and Snakeweed Studios - is set to be held on July 21 at The Promontory @ Marina Bay.
The fest will feature six home-grown bands like In Each Hand A Cutlass, Monster Cat and The Great Spy Experiment.
Singer-songwriter Kellie Lloyd from Australia will also be performing. Attendance to the fest is free. For more details, visit www.facebook.com/events/ 272121666228802
