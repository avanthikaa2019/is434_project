A conman was jailed 15 months yesterday for duping his 11 victims into signing up for mobile phone contracts and then selling their new handsets for his personal gain.
Syed Abdul Nazahar Syed Abdul Muhamad, 25 pleaded guilty to four counts of cheating. Eight other similar charges were taken into consideration.
Between May last year and January, he made away with 25 iPhones, 3 HTC smartphones and an iPad, worth a total of S$28,804. He met the victims through a chatline and accompanied them to sign up for new lines and phones as part of a fictitious survey on the telcos' services.
