Indonesia's Ahmad Sukran Jamjami added to his 3m springboard win last Saturday, pairing up with Andriyan to win the men's synchronised 3m springboard diving event with a total of 309.66 points at the South-east Asia Swimming Championships at Toa Payoh Swimming Complex.
In the women's 3m synchronised springboard event, Jasmine Lai and Kam Ling Kar of Malaysia took top honours with 245.43 points.
