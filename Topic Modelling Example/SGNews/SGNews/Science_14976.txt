Starhub cable-TV users who have the HD Interactive set-top box will now be able to record shows with the new Smart TVTM service.
While the service costs $4.28 per month, you will also have to buy an external hard drive for $139 from StarHub retailers in order to use the service.
There is a promotional price of $88 for the hard drive, which can store up to 160 hours' worth of standard content. Visit www. starhub.com/smarttv for more information
