A 50-year-old man has been arrested in connection with a series of shop theft cases at various supermarkets located in Fusionopolis, Vivocity and along Orchard Road.
The man is believed to be a serial shoplifter who took eight bottles of wine and whiskey worth $550 from a supermarket in Fudionopolis in May.
Investigations revealed that the same man was spotted in closed-circuit television footage captured at other supermarkets which also reported items missing. Police arrested the suspect in Toa Payoh on June 28.
He will face the court on charges of theft on June 30.
If convicted, he could be sentenced to seven years imprisonment and fined.
