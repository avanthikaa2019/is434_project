SINGAPORE - Applications for preview and actual-day tickets to this year's National Day Parade (NDP) are open from today to May 20.
Singaporeans and permanent residents can choose to hold a ballot for two, four or six tickets to either the NDP preview on Aug 4, or the parade on Aug 9.
The fewer the number of tickets applied for, the better the chance of obtaining them.
Visit ndp.org.sg for more details, or call 1800-637-2012.
