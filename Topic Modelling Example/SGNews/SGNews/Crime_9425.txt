Nanyang Siang Pau reported that a 60-year-old woman was among 15 dancers nabbed during a surprise raid on three entertainment outlets in Kuala Lumpur on Sunday.
The women, said to be clad only in bikini tops, were performing on stage when the three outlets were raided by Federal Terri&shy;tory Islamic Religious Department personnel.
The daily learnt that each dancer was paid RM300 (S$122) for the 10-minute performance.
