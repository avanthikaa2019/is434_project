The newspaper also reported Hong Kong superstar Andy Lau finally confirming the birth of his baby girl through his official fan page on Tuesday.
"(They are) discharged today. (We) have reached home. Thanks for all the blessings. Mother and daughter are healthy. Happy!" Lau posted online.
Lau also said he was trying to be a good father and husband.
A lot of his fans posted messages congratulating him.
Lau's Malaysian wife Carol Chu gave birth to a baby girl on May 8 in Hong Kong.
However, Lau did not post photos of his baby or disclose her name.
