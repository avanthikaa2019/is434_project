An SMRT train stalled at Paya Lebar MRT station at around 3.15pm on Sunday.
About 500 passengers at the station were affected
Passengers on the stalled train were asked to alight at the station.
A second train had to push the stalled train out.
Passengers on the second train may have felt some jerking during the incident.
SMRT said that MRT services along the eastbound line resumed about 45 minutes later at 4pm.
