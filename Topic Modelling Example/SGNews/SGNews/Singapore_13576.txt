SINGAPORE - Minister for Information, Communications and the Arts Dr Yaacob Ibrahim will be visiting the cities of Hong Kong, Shenzhen and San Francisco from May 7 to 13, 2012.
He will be visiting leading companies in the infocommunications technology and media sectors to discuss developments and explore collaboration opportunities.
Dr Yaacob will also take the opportunity to meet Singaporeans studying or working in the San Francisco vicinity.
He will be accompanied by officials from the Ministry of Information, Communications and the Arts and the Infocomm Development Authority of Singapore.
