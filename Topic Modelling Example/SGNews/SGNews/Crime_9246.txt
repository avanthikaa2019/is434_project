SHAH ALAM - A technician was charged at the Sessions Court yesterday with two counts of raping a minor.
Mohd Tarmizi Juwayer, 26, claimed trial to raping the 14-year-old girl at Mutiara Inn, in Taman Perindustrian Axis, Section 25 here at 11pm on July 29 last year and 5am the following day.
Judge Slamat Yahya granted him RM12,000 (S$4,800) bail with one surety for both the charges and fixed July 27 for mention.
He ordered Tarmizi to report to the nearest police station on the first day of every month and prohibited him from harassing the victim.
