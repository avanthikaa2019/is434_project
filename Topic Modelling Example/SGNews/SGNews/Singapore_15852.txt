JOHOR BARU - A 41-year-old Singaporean woman fell to her death from the fourth floor of a shopping complex in Kluang after getting into a heated argument with her Malay&shy;sian husband.
Ng Poh Wah, from Taman Gunung Lambak, was found dead after she fell from the Kluang Mall yesterday.
Kluang police chief Asst Comm Abdul Majid Ali said initial investigations revealed that there was no criminal element to the incident but police were still investigating.
"We discovered a note in the victim's pocket informing the person who found it to contact a phone number," he said.
