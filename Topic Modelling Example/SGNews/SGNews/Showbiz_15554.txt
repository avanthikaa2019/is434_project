HIT film The Hunger Games was the top-earning movie for a third weekend in a row in North America, scoring US$33.5 million (S$42 million) in theatres across North America, industry estimates showed on Sunday.
The movie blockbuster, which opened with a record US$152.5 million two weekends ago, raised its total to US$302.8 million, box-office tracker Exhibitor Relations said on its website. Globally, the film starring Jennifer Lawrence has pulled in US$460 million. The movie is based on the popular books by novelist Suzanne Collins.
