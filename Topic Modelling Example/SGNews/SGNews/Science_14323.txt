PETALING JAYA, Malaysia - The local prostitution racket that has been using Twitter to target foreign visitors has gone underground.
On the day the news was highlighted, which revealed the Twitter vice activities on Monday, both Twitter accounts offering "high-class social escorts" to foreigners had been deleted.
It had been reported that the local vice syndicate had been actively using Twitter to offer "high-class social escorts" to foreign visitors with the same pool of call girls sent to spas and private clubs.
One of the accounts, opened in May 2011, had tweeted 298 times.
It followed 1,564 people and was followed by 303.
