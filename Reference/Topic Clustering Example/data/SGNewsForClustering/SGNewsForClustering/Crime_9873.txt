A 32-year-old man has been arrested in connection with a series of Marsiling and Woodlands break-ins between May 3 and May 19.
The man is suspected of being involved in a total of six break-ins where visual and audio equipment were stolen from homes undergoing renovation.
In all cases, victims reported the number locks on their doors or gates missing.
Police officers found various audio and visual equipment and electrical appliances, which are believed to be stolen, from the suspect's residence.
He will face the court on charges of 'housebreaking and theft by day' on June 22.
If found guilty, he may be sentenced to a maximum of 10 years in jail and fined.
